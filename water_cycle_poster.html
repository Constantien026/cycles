The Water Cycle

Earth's Continuous Journey of Water Through Land, Sky & Ocean

☀️
01
Evaporation
Solar heat energy converts liquid water from oceans, lakes, and rivers into water vapor, which rises into the atmosphere.
☁️
02
Condensation
Rising water vapor cools at high altitudes and condenses around tiny dust particles, forming clouds and fog.
🌧️
03
Precipitation
When cloud droplets combine and grow too heavy, they fall back to Earth as rain, snow, sleet, or hail depending on temperature.
🏞️
04
Runoff
Precipitation that is not absorbed by the soil flows downhill over land into streams, rivers, lakes, and ultimately the ocean.
🌱
05
Infiltration
Water seeps down through soil and rock layers to recharge underground aquifers, which may later feed springs and wells.
🌿
06
Transpiration
Plants absorb groundwater through their roots and release it as water vapor through tiny pores (stomata) in their leaves.
